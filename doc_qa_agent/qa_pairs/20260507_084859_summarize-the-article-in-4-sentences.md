## Summary

The article argues that solo founders can replace their first three hires by building AI agents using tools like Claude and MCP servers. The three agents described are: a **Research Agent** that monitors competitors and delivers weekly intelligence briefs, a **Content Agent** that handles the full content lifecycle from ideation to publishing, and an **Operations Agent** that manages email triage, meeting prep, and weekly reporting. The agents become most powerful when connected through a shared knowledge base, allowing them to work as a coordinated team rather than isolated tools. While AI agents won't fully replace human judgment, they can cover 70-80% of what those three hires would do, at a fraction of the $180,000 annual cost of three full-time employees.
